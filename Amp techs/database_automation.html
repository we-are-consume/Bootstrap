Database Automation

Don’t compromise innovation for compliance

Today’s digital enterprises rely on high performing databases to drive faster, smarter applications. However, the complexity of managing a heterogeneous environment coupled with expanding compliance requirements and the severity of security threats burdens administrators. The good news is you don’t have to compromise compliance for speed. Amplified Technologies Database Automation helps database operations teams to provision, patch, configure, and maintain databases in a fully automated fashion and ensure databases start off and remain highly compliant.

  • Automate compliance checks, reducing >100 steps down to a few minutes of effort.
  • Rapidly harden databases from a variety of vendors, saving man hours.
  • Improve service by reducing downtime and resolving issues faster.
  • Manage risk by enforcing new controls and policies.
  • Establish and maintain your infrastructure with accuracy and ease.

    • Gain more agility and lower risk without adding staff

    Automating your database tasks will reduce risk, ease the burden of audits, improve consistency, and break up time-consuming bottlenecks – and you don’t have to add staff to make it a reality. Database Automation breaks down knowledge silos so expensive specialists can shift work to generalists to manage.

    Full Lifecycle Compliance

    Gold standard templates deliver security from day one and keep databases compliant. Out-of-the-box content for regulatory mandates like PCI-DSS and HIPAA along with security standards like DISA and CIS are included.

    Manage databases from a variety of vendors

    For Oracle, SQL, DB2, Sybase, MySQL, set standards once and then scale them out, lowering overall costs.
