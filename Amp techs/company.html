About Us

Registered in Nigeria with the main focus on making noticeable impact in I.C.T, Amplified Technologies has been empowering both consumers and developers alike in the field of Information and Communication Technology for almost a decade to enhance better performance by simply making codes work for all (ranging from custom response format to cryptographically strong algorithms and Pseudorandom sequences).

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a highly sort-after I.C.T Solution Provider not only in Nigeria but in Africa as a whole.

Our Mission

  • To help our clients deploy the right hardware and software solutions that best serve their unique environment.
  • To deploy I.C.T Policy into offices and business environments with aim of cutting unnecessary costs, improving efficiency and offering value to their clients or consumers.
  • To help our clients integrate the benefit of new I.C.T advancements into their homes, businesses and organisations.
